Sawren Construction (Pty) Ltd
Civil Engineering and Building Construction Company, Port Elizabeth

Sawren Construction is a Civil Engineering and Building Construction Company based in Port Elizabeth that offers its clients a variety of services within the civil and building contracting industry.

The company is committed to achieving excellence in maintaining and expanding on its position as one of the industry’s best contractors. In pursuit of this goal, Sawren Construction performs its services with the highest of integrity in a way that exceeds clients’ needs and expectations.

 

 

Sawren Construction offers the following services:

  • Earthworks
  • Concrete Works
  • Paving
  • Drainage Systems and Reticulation
  • Fencing
  • Building Works

Although based in Port Elizabeth in the Eastern Cape, Sawren Construction has satellite offices in Johannesburg and Hermanus and has worked for clients all over South Africa.
