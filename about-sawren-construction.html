Sawren Civil Engineering and Building Construction Company

Sawren Construction is a Civil Engineering and Building Construction Company offering a variety of services within the civil and building contracting industry.

Although based in Port Elizabeth in the Eastern Cape, Sawren Construction has satellite offices in Johannesburg and Hermanus and has worked for clients all over South Africa.

Since the company was registered in 1994, much of Sawren’s work has been focused on civil, building, earthworks and fencing projects at various Eskom substations throughout South Africa.

Sawren’s portfolio has also expanded to include the construction of 500 classrooms in the former Kwandebele and approximately 800 low-cost housing units in Tshikhota in the Limpopo Province to name a few.

Sawren Construction has been awarded Level Two BEE status and has a CIDB rating of 6CEPE.

The company’s stable workforce is comprised of 13 contracted employees, many of whom have been with the company since its inception. Sawren Construction also employs an extra 20 staff to work on the company’s contracts.
